FAQ

Questions, answered.

Do I need any technical skills?

No. If you can use Instagram, you can run ORBYT. Setup is a 60-second chat on your phone.

What languages does the AI speak?

Spanish, English, German, French, Italian, Dutch and Arabic — auto-detected per customer.

Can it really replace a receptionist?

For most service businesses, yes — for bookings, FAQs, follow-ups and reminders. You stay in control: anything ORBYT isn't sure about gets handed to you.

Is my customer data safe?

Yes. Hosted on Supabase + Vercel in the EU, GDPR-aligned, encrypted at rest and in transit. We never sell data.

What if I want to cancel?

One tap in settings. No call, no email, no friction. You keep your data export.
