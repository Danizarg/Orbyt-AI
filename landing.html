Frequently asked questions

Do my customers know they're talking to an AI? +
Only if you want them to. You can train the AI to use your business name and speak exactly like you. Most businesses find customers don't notice — they just appreciate the fast response.
What languages does Orbyt AI support? +
Orbyt AI automatically detects and responds in the customer's language. Spanish, English, Catalan, French, German — it handles them all without any setup.
How long does setup take? +
Most businesses are fully set up in under 10 minutes. Create your account, connect your channels, and tell the AI about your business. That's it.
Can I see what the AI is saying to my customers? +
Yes — your dashboard shows every conversation in real time. You can also turn off auto-send and review AI drafts before they're sent.
What if I want to cancel? +
Cancel anytime from your account settings. No contracts, no cancellation fees. You keep access until the end of your billing period.
Is my customer data safe? +
Yes. All data is encrypted and stored securely. We never sell or share your data or your customers' data with anyone.
